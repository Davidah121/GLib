Construct a new GuiScrollBar object It creates a horizontal or vertical scroll bar. By default, it is vertical. The scrolling area will be sized according to the startX,startY,endX,endY values. The button for scrolling will be based on that. Can use up to 3 GuiRectangleButtons as children.

Parameters
startXStart x of the scrolling area
startYStart y of the scrolling area
endXEnd x of the scrolling area
endYEnd y of the scrolling area
